Public BBoard
You can also subscribe to the CMU Public Chess Club BBoard in Pine or Mulberry,

assocs.chessclub

If you send email to

bb+assocs.chessclub@andrew.cmu.edu

no member will get the email, but your message will be posted on the chess bboard, just like cmu.misc.market.
Feel free to post any interesting chess news on the bboard!

The mailing list and bboard are moderated by Chess Club officers.
