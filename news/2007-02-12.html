See the Chess Club this Friday at the UC!

The CMU Chess Club has reserved a spot in UC Fridays this friday, February 16, just for you! You will be able to enter tournaments offering Amazon gift card prices or just play relaxed walk-in games.

Everyone's invited! From experts to novices, we have someone at your skill level to play a game with you! We may even offer a tutorial session for those interested.

So remember to keep an eye out for us, 4:30 to 6:30 in Kirr Commons. Oh, and of course, free food and drinks while supplies last!
